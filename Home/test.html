Advantages

The Potential of Rice Straw for Biogas Production

  • Utilizes raw materials from a combination system of husks, rice, and animal waste.
  • Does not cause waste because the output besides gas, namely husk solids, can be used as plant fertilizer
  • Has a durability of up to 3 months

VISSION & MISSION

The Potential of Rice Straw for Biogas Production

  • Optimize biogas production efficiency by combining rice husk and animal manure
  • Reducing dependence on LPG, minimizing air pollution, and creating an eco-friendly circular economy.
  • Develop household-scale biogas technology using Solid State Anaerobic Digestion (SS-AD)
  • Promote public awareness of renewable energy use and sustainable waste management
